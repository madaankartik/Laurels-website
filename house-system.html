House System

Fostering Leadership, Teamwork and Healthy Competition

About Our House System

The House System at Laurels International School is designed to promote leadership, teamwork and healthy competition among students. Each student is assigned to one of four houses upon admission, creating a sense of belonging and school spirit. The houses compete throughout the year in various academic, sports and cultural activities.

The Four Houses
Shaurya House

Motto: "Courage in Every Step"

Color: Red

Values: Bravery, Integrity, Determination

Shaurya House inspires its members to face challenges fearlessly and uphold strong moral values. It fosters leaders who are bold in action and steadfast in principles.

Veer House

Motto: "Strength with Honor"

Color: Blue

Values: Resilience, Responsibility, Discipline

Veer House celebrates inner strength and the will to persevere. Its members carry themselves with dignity, showing that true power lies in self-control and honor.

Yodha House

Motto: "Fight for What’s Right"

Color: Green

Values: Justice, Unity, Perseverance

Yodha House stands for the spirit of a warrior who protects and serves. Its members unite to defend fairness, always striving to overcome obstacles together.

Tejas House

Motto: "Illuminate the Path"

Color: Yellow

Values: Knowledge, Innovation, Excellence

Tejas House symbolizes brilliance and enlightenment. Its members lead through creativity and learning, inspiring others to achieve their highest potential.

House Points System
How Points Are Awarded:
  • Academic Excellence: 10-50 points per achievement
  • Sports Victories: 5-30 points per win
  • Cultural Activities: 5-25 points per participation
  • Leadership Roles: 10-20 points per role
  • Community Service: 5-15 points per activity
Annual Awards:
  • Best House Trophy: Highest total points
  • Academic Excellence: Best academic performance
  • Sports Champion: Best sports performance
  • Cultural Excellence: Best cultural activities
  • Leadership Award: Outstanding leadership
